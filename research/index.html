Research

My two (main) topics of interest are Quantile Regression and Topological Data Analysis.

During my PhD this was materialized in what follows:

  • Model-based Quantile Regression for Discrete Data

  • Supervised Learning with Indefinite Topological Kernels

  • Persistence Flamelets: multiscale Persistent Homology for kernel density exploration



Now I am working on

  • Regression trees for mixed type data (including Topological summaries!)

  • Wasserstein distance for sample size determination, with applications to clinical trials

but nothing has seen the light yet.



My fear of commitment forces me to study also other, unrelated, things.
